Springdale is a housing project designed for people who appreciate fine living. Everything at Springdale, from the stand-out design to the quality of construction, is intended to make you happier in the most important place in your life - your home

Springdale is located in Roadpali, the most exciting new hub in Navi Mumbai. Roadpali falls between Kalamboli and the upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport. It is an urban neighbourhood planned along the south bank of the Kasadi River. You will fall in love with the huge expanse of green in the area!

Residential property consist of 2.5 BHK plus Terrace apartments in tower with Stilt + Podium +18 Residential floors. In keeping with modern town planning standards for a better quality of life, Roadpali has large spaces for gardens, parks, sports infrastructures, educational institutions and healthcare facilities.
